Idea
The Idea is pretty simple - to perfect your English through debate. Debates are a great chance to voice an opinion, express yourself freely in public, deliver arguments, simply to be heard or just to listen. Advanced English level opens a lot of unique opportunities all around the world.
Of course, debates and English can go separate routes easily, but its combination will help you to polish both. In addition to all the obvious advantages you can imagine from such a blend, our team will ensure that you get other benefits such as:
  • you will be observed and guided by professional English tutors and a native speaker;
  • you will receive a personal "record card" with language corrections for improvement;
  • your presentation and language skills will be evaluated;
  • you will overcome the fear of speaking;
  • you will meet a lot of new interesting people;
  • you will have a lot of fun;
  • you name it!
If you are still asking yourself the question "Is such a performance for me?", it should be mentioned that debating in English is useful for:
  • anyone who loves English;
  • anyone who wants to practice speaking English;
  • anyone who wants to support and improve the English level they already have;
  • anyone who pursue IELTS, TOEFL and other ESL exams;
  • companies, corporations and small firms for professional staff development;
  • team-building in order to unite your employees;
  • native speakers who seek rest and communication in great company;
Location
We hold debates at various places, for example, they can be public, like co-working areas, cafés etc, or private ones like offices, schools and so on. Our next event is held in Ziferblat open space.
In order to know where our next debate takes place subscribe to our Facebook page or come back to the website, we update information on a weekly basis. In case of questions - feel free to contact us.
